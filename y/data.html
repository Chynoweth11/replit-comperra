Data Usage & Sources

Our Data Philosophy

Comperra is committed to providing accurate, comprehensive, and up-to-date building materials data. We source information directly from manufacturers, authorized distributors, and verified industry databases to ensure the highest quality data for our users.

Data Sources

Primary Sources

  • Manufacturer product catalogs and specifications
  • Authorized distributor databases
  • Industry certification bodies (TCNA, NWFA, etc.)
  • Testing laboratory reports
  • Professional installation guidelines

Partner Integration

  • Direct API connections with major manufacturers
  • Real-time pricing feeds from suppliers
  • Inventory and availability updates
  • New product announcements and updates

Data Quality Assurance

Verification Process

  • Cross-reference multiple sources for accuracy
  • Regular updates from manufacturer feeds
  • Manual verification of critical specifications
  • User feedback integration and corrections

Update Frequency

  • Product specifications: Weekly updates
  • Pricing information: Daily refreshes
  • Inventory status: Real-time where available
  • New products: As announced by manufacturers

Availability & Supply

Data Accuracy & Disclaimers

Accuracy Standards

While we maintain high standards for data accuracy, building materials information can change frequently. We recommend verifying critical specifications directly with manufacturers.

Data Limitations

  • Pricing may vary by location and quantity
  • Specifications subject to manufacturer changes
  • Availability depends on regional distribution
  • Some products may have limited data availability
