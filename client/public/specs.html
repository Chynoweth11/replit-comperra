Find Materials by Technical Specifications

Search our database using specific technical criteria to find materials that meet your project requirements.

Common Tile Specifications

  • PEI Rating: Durability classification (1-5)
  • Water Absorption: Porosity measurement
  • DCOF: Dynamic Coefficient of Friction
  • Frost Resistance: Freeze-thaw performance
  • Thermal Shock: Temperature change resistance
  • Chemical Resistance: Stain and chemical durability

Stone & Slab Specifications

  • Compressive Strength: Load-bearing capacity
  • Flexural Strength: Bending resistance
  • Absorption Rate: Porosity and sealing needs
  • Hardness: Mohs scale rating
  • Thermal Conductivity: Heat transfer properties
  • Edge Profiles: Finishing options available

LVT Performance Criteria

  • Wear Layer: Thickness in mils (6-40 mil)
  • Overall Thickness: Total product thickness
  • Indentation Resistance: Furniture load capacity
  • Dimensional Stability: Temperature expansion
  • Sound Rating: Impact and acoustic performance
  • Installation Method: Click, glue, or loose lay

Hardwood Classifications

  • Janka Hardness: Dent resistance rating
  • Grade: Select, #1 Common, #2 Common
  • Moisture Content: Stability measurement
  • Plank Width: Narrow, wide, or mixed widths
  • Finish Type: Pre-finished or site-finished
  • Installation Grade: Above, on, or below grade
