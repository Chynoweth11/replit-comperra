Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Comperra collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our building materials comparison platform.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Project details and material preferences
  • Location information (ZIP code, city, state)
  • Company or professional information
  • Communications and correspondence

Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage data and platform interactions
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and general location
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Search queries and material comparisons

Third-Party Information

  • Information from manufacturer and supplier partners
  • Professional verification data
  • Industry directories and databases

How We Use Your Information

  • Platform Services: Provide material comparisons, pricing, and recommendations
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Business Connections: Connect you with suppliers and manufacturers
  • Platform Improvement: Analyze usage to enhance features and functionality
  • Marketing: Send relevant updates and industry information (with consent)
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and protect our rights

Information Sharing

With Your Consent

  • Authorized dealers and suppliers for pricing requests
  • Manufacturers for product information and support
  • Professional network connections

Service Providers

  • Technology infrastructure and hosting services
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer communication platforms
  • Payment processing (if applicable)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, to protect our rights, or to ensure platform security and user safety.

Data Security

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Secure access controls and authentication measures
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Limited access to personal information on need-to-know basis
  • Incident response procedures for potential security breaches

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in portable format

Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Choose communication frequency and methods
  • Manage notification preferences

Cookies and Tracking

  • Manage cookie preferences through browser settings
  • Opt out of analytics and tracking services
  • Control targeted advertising preferences

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Information: Until account deletion or extended inactivity
  • Transaction Records: As required by applicable laws and regulations
  • Communications: For reasonable periods to provide ongoing support
  • Analytics Data: In aggregated, anonymized form for platform improvement

International Users

Comperra operates from the United States. If you access our platform from other countries:

  • Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States
  • We provide appropriate safeguards for international data transfers
  • You consent to such transfers by using our platform
  • Local privacy laws may provide additional rights and protections

Children's Privacy

Comperra is designed for professional and adult use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make material changes:

  • We will notify you via email or platform notification
  • The updated policy will be posted on our website
  • The "Last updated" date will be revised
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
