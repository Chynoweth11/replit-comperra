Frequently Asked Questions

What is Comperra?

Comperra is a comprehensive platform for comparing building materials including tiles, stone slabs, LVT, hardwood, heating systems, and carpet. We provide detailed specifications, pricing information, and expert guidance to help you make informed material selection decisions.

How accurate is the pricing information?

Our pricing data is sourced directly from manufacturers and authorized distributors. Prices are updated regularly, but we recommend contacting suppliers directly for final quotes as prices can vary by location, quantity, and current market conditions.

Can I purchase materials directly through Comperra?

Comperra is a comparison and information platform. We don't sell materials directly, but we can connect you with authorized dealers and distributors in your area. Use our "Get Pricing" feature to request quotes from local suppliers.

How do I compare products from different categories?

Our platform maintains strict category separation to ensure accurate comparisons. You can compare tiles with other tiles, slabs with other slabs, etc. This ensures you're comparing materials with similar applications and specifications.

What information do you provide about each material?

For each material, we provide detailed specifications including dimensions, durability ratings (like PEI for tiles), water absorption rates, recommended applications, installation requirements, maintenance guidelines, and pricing information.

How can I add products to my comparison?

Use the comparison checkboxes next to product names, or use our URL scraping feature to add products from manufacturer websites. You can compare multiple products side-by-side with detailed specification tables.

Do you provide installation guidance?

Yes, our buying guides include installation considerations, requirements, and best practices for each material type. However, we always recommend consulting with professional installers for specific project requirements.

How often is the product data updated?

We continuously update our product database with new materials, specifications, and pricing. Our web scraping tools help us maintain current information from manufacturer websites and authorized sources.

Can contractors and designers use Comperra for projects?

Absolutely. Comperra is designed for professionals including contractors, interior designers, architects, and builders. Our detailed specifications and comparison tools help streamline material selection for projects of any size.

Is there an API available for developers?

We're developing API access for integrations and bulk data needs. Contact us at partnerships@comperra.com to discuss API access and partnership opportunities.

How do I request pricing for materials?

Use the "Get Pricing" buttons on product pages to submit a pricing request. We'll forward your information to authorized dealers in your area who can provide accurate quotes based on your specific needs and location.

What if I can't find a specific product or brand?

We're constantly expanding our database. If you can't find a specific product or brand, contact us with the details and we'll work to add it to our platform. You can also use our URL scraping feature to add products directly from manufacturer websites.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.
