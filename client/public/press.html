About Comperra

Comperra is revolutionizing how professionals and homeowners select building materials by providing the industry's most comprehensive comparison platform. Our technology-driven approach combines verified product data, real-time pricing, and expert guidance to streamline material selection for projects of any scale.

Founded in 2025, Comperra serves contractors, interior designers, architects, and homeowners across the United States, offering detailed comparisons of tiles, stone slabs, LVT, hardwood, heating systems, and carpet from leading manufacturers.
